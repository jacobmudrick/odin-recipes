Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

  • Mac and cheese box
  • Milk
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Mix the cooked noodles with the other things
  4. Eat it from the pot with a big wooden spoon
  5. Chef's note: Stir the noodles around a lot if the sound bothers people!
